About me

I am Andreas Jemstedt, a cognitive psychologist working as a senior lecturer at Stockholm University's Department of Education. I specialize in evidence-based methods to improve learning and education. My research areas include learning, metacognition, and technology-enhanced learning. I utilize quantitative methods in my research and constantly aim to improve the research methods I use. Additionally, I am dedicated to teaching and academic development, and I have taught courses and workshops in psychology, statistics, AI, and research methods at Stockholm University.

Beside my day job, I am a freelance learning specialist, educational developer, and data scientist. I have worked with a clients at Karolinska Institutet, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, and the Government Offices of Sweden.
